# Standard Customer Due Diligence (CDD) Policy

## Ultimate Beneficial Ownership (UBO)
* [cite_start]**Identification Threshold**: The institution must identify any natural person who ultimately owns or controls a legal entity through direct or indirect ownership of 25% or more of the shares or voting rights[cite: 8].
* **Verification**: For each identified UBO, the onboarding analyst must obtain valid government-issued photographic identification, proof of address, and verify their identity using independent, reliable sources.

## Core Corporate Verification Data
To establish a business relationship, the following corporate attributes must be gathered:
1. Legal Entity Name and Registered Trade Name.
2. Principal place of business operations.
3. Certificate of Incorporation or equivalent official registry extract.
4. Board resolution authorizing the opening of the account and designating authorized signatories.
