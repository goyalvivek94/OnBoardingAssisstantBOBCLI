# Sanctions and PEP Screening Protocol

## Real-Time Screening Requirements
[cite_start]All clients, authorized signers, and UBOs must be screened against major international lists (OFAC, European Union, United Nations, HMT) prior to the activation of any account functions[cite: 14].

## Dispositions of Alerts
When a potential match occurs, the analyst must categorize the alert into one of two options:

* [cite_start]**False Positive**: The name matches, but metadata fields (such as Date of Birth, Nationality, or Country of Residence) prove the client is completely distinct from the target on the sanctions list[cite: 14]. The alert must be documented and suppressed with a clear rationale.
* [cite_start]**True Match**: Metadata confirms the client is an exact match to a sanctioned individual or entity[cite: 14]. The compliance team must block the onboarding process immediately, freeze any funds if necessary, and submit a report to relevant authorities (e.g., OFAC) within regulatory deadlines.
