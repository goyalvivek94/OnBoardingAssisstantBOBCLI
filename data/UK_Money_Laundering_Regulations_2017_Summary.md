# UK Money Laundering Regulations 2017 Summary

## Risk-Based Approach
[cite_start]The core pillar of the MLR 2017 is the requirement to establish, maintain, and review a business-wide risk assessment that accurately determines the business's vulnerability to money laundering and terrorist financing[cite: 12].

## Customer Verification Standards
* **Independent Data Sources**: Identity verification cannot rely solely on client-provided documents. [cite_start]Analysts must cross-reference data points using independent electronic verification platforms or company house registries[cite: 12].
* **Timing**: Verification of identity must take place *before* the establishment of the business relationship or the execution of an occasional transaction.
