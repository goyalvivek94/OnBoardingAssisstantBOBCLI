# USA PATRIOT Act Section 326: Customer Identification Program (CIP)

## Minimum Mandatory Information
[cite_start]For every U.S. person or entity opening an account, the institution must collect the following four critical pieces of identification before account creation[cite: 13]:
1. [cite_start]**Name**: Full legal name of the individual or registered corporate entity[cite: 13].
2. [cite_start]**Date of Birth**: For individuals[cite: 13].
3. [cite_start]**Address**: A physical residential or principal place of business street address (P.O. Boxes are strictly prohibited)[cite: 13].
4. [cite_start]**Identification Number**: A Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN/SSN) for U.S. entities/citizens, or a passport number/alien identification card number for non-U.S. individuals[cite: 13].

## Verification Timeline
The identity must be verified using documentary or non-documentary methods within a reasonable time *after* account opening, but prior to allowing full transactional access.
