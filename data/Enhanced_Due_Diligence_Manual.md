# Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) Manual for High-Risk Clients

## High-Risk Triggers
[cite_start]Enhanced Due Diligence is mandatory for any client operating in a high-risk sector (e.g., Crypto Assets, Gambling, Defense) or flagged via geographic or political exposure risk factors[cite: 9, 10].

## Source of Wealth (SoW) vs. Source of Funds (SoF)
* [cite_start]**Source of Wealth (SoW)**: Refers to the origin of the client's entire accumulated net worth (e.g., corporate inheritance, sale of property, long-term business profits)[cite: 9]. Documentation must trace back at least 5 to 10 years to prove legitimacy.
* [cite_start]**Source of Funds (SoF)**: Refers to the origin of the specific funds being used for the transactions or initial deposit within this relationship (e.g., bank transfer from a specific corporate account, documented loan)[cite: 9].

## Politically Exposed Persons (PEPs)
* [cite_start]**Definition**: Individuals entrusted with prominent public functions, their immediate family members, and close associates[cite: 9].
* **Approval Workflow**: Onboarding a PEP requires explicit, written sign-off from Senior Management or the Chief Compliance Officer (CCO).
