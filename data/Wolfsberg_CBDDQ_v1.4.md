# Wolfsberg Correspondent Banking Due Diligence Questionnaire (CBDDQ) v1.4

## Overview
This document outlines the core compliance metrics required to evaluate downstream financial institution relationships. The goal is to mitigate nesting risks and establish absolute transparency across cross-border clearing channels.

## Cross-Border & Nesting Risk
* **Downstream Relationships**: The responding institution must declare if it offers downstream correspondent banking services to third-party financial institutions.
* **Nesting Identification**: Procedures must be in place to ensure downstream "nested" accounts are not being utilized by unauthorized money service businesses (MSBs) or unregulated offshore banks.
* **Shell Banks**: Under no circumstances does the institution maintain relationships with shell banks or permit shell banks to utilize its accounts.

## Payment Transparency
* **SWIFT MT/MX Messages**: Full originator and beneficiary information (Name, Address, Account Number) must be populated in all payment messages (e.g., MT103, incoming/outgoing ISO 20022 messages).
* **Straight-Through Processing (STP)**: Payments flagged for missing fields must be systematically held for manual compliance review before execution.
