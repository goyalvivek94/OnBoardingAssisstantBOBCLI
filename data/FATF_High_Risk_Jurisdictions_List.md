# FATF High-Risk and Other Monitored Jurisdictions Handling Protocol

## The Blacklist (High-Risk Jurisdictions)
* [cite_start]**Definition**: Countries with significant strategic deficiencies in their regimes to counter money laundering, terrorist financing, and financing of proliferation[cite: 11].
* **Action Required**: Apply strict countermeasures and Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD). Countermeasures include prohibiting the establishment of branches or subsidiaries in the country and strictly restricting financial transactions with entities inside the jurisdiction.

## The Greylist (Jurisdictions Under Increased Monitoring)
* [cite_start]**Definition**: Countries actively working with the FATF to address strategic deficiencies[cite: 11].
* **Action Required**: Apply enhanced monitoring tailored to the specific deficiencies identified by the FATF. Onboarding teams must increase the frequency of transaction monitoring and refresh CDD data annually.
