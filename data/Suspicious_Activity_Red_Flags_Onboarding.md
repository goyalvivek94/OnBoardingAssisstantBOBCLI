# Suspicious Activity Red Flags During Onboarding

## Behavior and Document Red Flags
The following indicators represent high risk or potential attempts to circumvent financial systems. [cite_start]Discovery of these anomalies requires an immediate Escalation Report[cite: 16]:

* [cite_start]**Reluctance to Provide Information**: The client is defensive, evasive, or explicitly reluctant to disclose UBO identities, source of wealth, or basic financial statements[cite: 16].
* [cite_start]**Unjustified Complexity**: The corporate structure spans multiple jurisdictions with zero clear commercial, tax, or operational justification[cite: 16].
* [cite_start]**Mismatched Verification Data**: Information provided on corporate registration records contradicts utility bills, financial footprints, or statements offered by the client's representative[cite: 16].
