# Complex Corporate Structure Verification Guide

## Deciphering Complex Entities
[cite_start]Onboarding multi-layered offshore corporate structures requires mapping out the entire ownership tree to identify the ultimate beneficiaries[cite: 15].

## Shell Companies & Economic Ownership
* [cite_start]**Voting Rights vs. Economic Ownership**: Analysts must differentiate between individuals holding voting power (control via board influence) and individuals holding economic ownership (entitled to capital profits)[cite: 15]. [cite_start]Both streams must be evaluated for the 25% ownership threshold[cite: 8].
* **Intermediary Layers**: If a client is owned by a holding company in a secrecy jurisdiction (e.g., Cayman Islands, British Virgin Islands), analysts must obtain certificates of incumbency and share registers for every single intermediary layer until a natural person or listed public entity is discovered.
